Best for Web:

WebP: Modern format, 25-35% smaller than PNG/JPG. Quality: 85-90%

JPG: Universal support, great for photos. Quality: 80-95%

PNG: Lossless, supports transparency. Perfect for logos/graphics. Quality: 100%

Quick Tips:

  • Website images: WebP @ 85% or JPG @ 80-85%
  • Logos/graphics: PNG @ 100%
  • Social media: JPG @ 80-85%
  • Grayscale: Converts image to black & white (saves as PNG)
  • Sepia: Vintage brown tone effect (saves as PNG)
  • Invert: Reverses all colors (saves as PNG)

ğŸµ Audio Format Guide

Lossless (Best Quality):

FLAC: Free Lossless. Perfect quality, ~50% smaller than WAV

WAV: Uncompressed. Massive files. Industry standard

Lossy (Compressed):

OGG: Free format, good quality

  • General listening: MP3 @ 80-90% quality
  • Podcasts: MP3 @ 60-70% quality
