How to Use

▶ Loading a File

Tap the big play button or anywhere in the dotted box to browse your files. You can load any video or audio file — MP4, MOV, MKV, MP3, WAV, M4A and more. On desktop you can also drag & drop directly onto the box.

🎵 Controls

Once loaded, use the standard player controls to play, pause, scrub, and adjust volume. On iPhone, playback also appears on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island so you can control it without opening the app.

📂 Loading a Different File

After a file is loaded, tap the "Load a different file" button at the bottom of the player to swap it out.

🎨 Backgrounds

Tap the ⚙️ gear icon in the top right to open the background selector. Choose from 12 animated backgrounds — Matrix, Tron, Starfield, Synthwave, Retro Grid, Neon Rain, Fireflies, Hex Grid, Glitch, Matrix II, and Aurora. Your choice is saved automatically.

🌤 Weather

The weather widget shows your current temperature, local time, conditions, and city. Allow location access when prompted for it to work.

🔄 Reload

Tap the logo in the top left to reload the app with a satisfying animation.
