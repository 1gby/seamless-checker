Stremio Addons (Stream Sources)

Add Stremio-compatible addons like Torrentio, Comet, MediaFusion, Peerflix, and TorrentsDB as stream sources. More addons = more cached results. Configure your debrid key in each addon for best results.

💡 Torrentio (RD) — The most popular Stremio addon for torrent streams. Aggregates results from multiple torrent providers into one feed. Go to torrentio.strem.fun/configure, set your RD API key, then copy the manifest URL and paste above.

💡 Comet — A fast, modern Stremio addon focused on debrid-cached torrents. Supports Real-Debrid, AllDebrid, Premiumize and more. Go to comet.feels.legal/configure, connect your debrid account, customise your filters, then copy the manifest URL and paste it in the field above.

💡 MediaFusion — A community-driven Stremio addon that indexes multiple torrent and streaming sources. Highly configurable with provider and quality filters. Go to mediafusion.elfhosted.com/configure, set your preferences and debrid provider, then copy the manifest URL and paste it above.

💡 Peerflix — A lightweight Stremio addon that finds cached torrents on your debrid service. Fast and efficient with minimal setup. Go to peerflix-addon.elfhosted.com/configure, connect your debrid account, then copy the manifest URL and paste it above.

💡 TorrentsDB — A torrent database addon that provides an extensive index of cached torrents from Real-Debrid and other debrid services. Go to torrents-db.elfhosted.com/configure, set up your debrid provider, then copy the manifest URL and paste it above.
